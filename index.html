Welcome to LncRTAR server

Long noncoding RNAs(lncRNAs) constitutes a class of noncoding RNAs which are versatile molecules and perform regulatory functions.
LncRTar involves development of a tool which predicts the interaction energy of query(lncRNA) and it's target(mRNA) with low computational cost.
It will also address all the limitations of the existing target prediction tools. Also it incorporates the algorithms that are specifically devised for lncRNA and mRNA target prediction.
